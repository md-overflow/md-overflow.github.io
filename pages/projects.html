Protocol Verification

AHB-to-APB Bridge — Verification IP (VIP)

Full UVM-based VIP for an AHB-to-APB bridge with separate AHB master and APB slave agents, each with driver, monitor, sequencer, and transaction classes. Supports SINGLE, INCR, and WRAP burst types with constrained-random stimulus. Scoreboard performs byte-accurate data comparison across all transfer sizes (byte, halfword, word), with functional covergroups on both AHB and APB sides. Regression Makefile targets Mentor QuestaSim and Synopsys VCS.

SystemVerilog UVM AMBA AHB/APB QuestaSim Synopsys VCS
